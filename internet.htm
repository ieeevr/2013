Internet Access

VR attendees will have access to wireless internet connectivity throughout the hotel as a component of the resort services package. This includes guest rooms and public areas such as meeting rooms, lobbies and lounges. Please be courteous to fellow conference attendees, and do not unnecessarily utilize high bandwidth applications, such as video streaming, while on the wireless connection. The main meeting rooms will also be equipped with a small number of wired ethernet connections for temporary robust/high-bandwidth needs.
