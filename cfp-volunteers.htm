Call for Student Volunteers

We are looking for dedicated and enthusiastic student volunteers to help "make it all happen." Volunteers commit to twenty hours of volunteer effort and will receive conference registration, proceedings, t-shirt and other items of appreciation.

Serving as a student volunteer is a great way to get involved with the conference, meet lots of people, and help out in a big way.

To apply for a student volunteer position, please fill out this form. The deadline for applications is December 20, 2012. We will send out notifications on January 18, 2013.

For more information, please contact the Student Volunteer Chairs:

  • Emiko Charbonneau, University of Central Florida
  • Joon Hao Chuah, University of Florida, USA
  • Shivashankar Halan, University of Florida, USA

studentvolunteerschairs [at] ieeevr.org
